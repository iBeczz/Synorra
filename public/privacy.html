Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Training Lab is a personal fitness dashboard built and used exclusively by its developer. It is not a public-facing product or service.

Who uses this app

This application is used solely by its developer. There are no other users, accounts, or registrations.

Data collected and how it is used

The dashboard connects to Strava and Whoop to read personal fitness data (activity records, recovery scores, sleep metrics, and heart rate data). This data is fetched on-demand and displayed only in the browser. It is not stored on any server, database, or third-party service beyond what Strava and Whoop themselves retain.

OAuth tokens (used to authenticate with Strava and Whoop) are stored locally in the browser's localStorage on the developer's own device. Whoop's client credentials are stored as environment variables on Netlify and are never exposed to the browser.

Data sharing

No data is shared with any third parties. No analytics, tracking scripts, or advertising services are present on this site.

Third-party services

This application connects to the following external services at the developer's direction:

Strava — activity and GPS data. Governed by Strava's Privacy Policy.
Whoop — recovery, sleep, and strain data. Governed by Whoop's Privacy Policy.
Netlify — hosting and serverless functions. Governed by Netlify's Privacy Policy.

Contact

As this is a personal tool, there is no support or contact mechanism. If you have reached this page in error, please navigate away.

Training Lab — personal use only.
